## How, What, Where, When, Who: Mechanisms for Creative Control

This talk examines how creative control in audiovisual production can be understood through the questions of how, what, where, when, and who. These dimensions structure any creation process, from algorithmic systems to traditional media such as watercolor on paper. The talk investigates computational mechanisms that support artistic intent and shows how they shape authorship, expression, and the aesthetic qualities that emerge.
