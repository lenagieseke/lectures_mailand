# Group Work Review Questions

## Questions about the Pipeline

Source
* Why did you choose this particular source? What properties of it are important for your system?
* What aspects of the phenomenon are lost when you measure it?
* What assumptions about the source are hidden in your design?

Capture / Encode
* What exactly does the system measure?
* What does the system not capture from the original phenomenon?
* How might noise or measurement error affect the system?

Transform / Model
* Why did you choose this transformation model instead of another one?
* What would happen if the model behaved differently?
* Which parameters control the behavior of your system?

  
Setup Overall
* If I changed only the model, would your world change dramatically?
	* If the answer is no, the translation might still be superficial.


## Questions about the Third Space

System Setup
* What entities exist in your system?
* What rules govern their behavior?
* What kinds of states or patterns can the system produce over time?
  
* Could the system produce outcomes that were not explicitly designed?
	* That reveals whether it is generative or just reactive.


Influence (feedback)
* How do participants affect the system?
* Does the system change how people behave?
* Could participants learn strategies to manipulate the system?
  
* If users interact for ten minutes, will their behavior change because of the system?


Meaning
* What does the translation say about the original phenomenon?
* Why is this mapping meaningful rather than arbitrary?
* Would the concept still work if you replaced the source with something else?
  
* Why does this mapping make sense conceptually?


## Questions about Design Choices

* What part of your pipeline has the strongest influence on the world you created?
* What would be the most interesting failure mode of your system?
* What would make the system more surprising or more generative?


## Questions about Aesthetics and Experience

* What would the audience feel when experiencing your system?
* Is the system more about spectacle or about conceptual meaning?
* How does the aesthetic design support the idea?


## Summary

* If I remove the concept, is the system still interesting?
* If I remove the system, is the concept still interesting?

Good projects usually survive at least one of those tests.

⸻

What to Look Out For, weak ideas often show:
* arbitrary mappings (“sound controls color”)
* no real system dynamics
* aesthetics without concept or concept without system

> Does this translation actually build a world, or does it just trigger effects?
